Label story

Two decades deep.

Vertigo Records started in Berlin in 2004 and has spent two decades building a psychedelic catalog — rooted in goa and psytrance, with a roster that now reaches into hybrid-live and techno. The archive is the proof: a long line of releases, compilations and anniversary editions.
