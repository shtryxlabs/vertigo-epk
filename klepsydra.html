The artist

klePSYdra is the psytrance project of Denys — DJ and current head of Vertigo Records (Berlin, est. 2004). The main set is full-power, crystal-clear main-time psychedelic for peak hours and main stages; on request, a funky morning / daytime psy-progressive set for sunrise floors.
